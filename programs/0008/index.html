This script displays all polycubes with 1, 2, 3, ..., 7 cubes which fit in a 3 × 3 × 3 bounding box. Click and drag to pan around the target, click on other polycubes to shift focus, scroll to zoom, and use arrow keys or hold right click to move left and right.
